PMP-Prep for Youth by YLDP

6-month leadership & project management journey for young changemakers (16-30)

Join the Program

About the Program

This program helps young people develop project thinking, leadership skills, and communication abilities using PMP principles adapted for youth. It combines the best approaches from project management, leadership, goal setting, and mentoring.

Program Structure

Time Management & Mission

Leaders learn how to set goals and manage time effectively.

Risk & Resource Management

Handling challenges and using resources wisely.

Problem Solving & Quality Assurance

Learning to solve problems and maintain high standards.

Empathy & Communication

Building strong connections and understanding others.

Sustainability & Resilience

Leading with responsibility for long-term impact.

Program Outcomes

Leadership Basics

Understanding what leadership means in real life.

Communication & Teamwork

Learning to work and speak effectively in teams.

Small Project Experience

Practice leading small tasks with mentor support.

Problem Solving

Identifying challenges and exploring solutions.

Networking

Connecting with peers from different countries.

Inspired by Alumni

Learning from real success stories of alumni.

Eligibility

  • Age: 16 to 30
  • No prior leadership training required
  • Motivated, curious, and open to learning
  • Comfortable with basic English
  • Passionate about making a difference
  • Subscription fee: $20/month

Our Mission

Not just a course, but a community of young leaders who want to think clearly, act systematically, and live with dignity. After graduation, you become part of the YLDP Alumni community, where growth continues.
