About PMB

Project Management Basics (PMB) is a 6-month online program combining adapted PMP principles, mentor support and practical projects. We focus on turning ideas into outcomes.

Our Mission

To empower young people with the skills, mindset and confidence to lead meaningful projects and create lasting impact.

Program Structure

💡 21st Century Skills

Creativity, critical thinking, collaboration, and digital literacy.

🕒 Time Management & Mission

Set priorities, create mission-driven plans, manage time effectively.

⚖ Risk & Resource Management

Identify risks, plan resources and handle constraints.

🧩 Problem Solving & Quality

Approach problems, validate solutions, maintain standards.

🤝 Empathy & Communication

Build teams, communicate clearly, lead with empathy.

🌱 Sustainability & Resilience

Create projects that last and adapt to change.
